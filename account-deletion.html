Account Deletion

Arc Astra Network allows users to permanently delete their account directly inside the mobile application. No email request is required.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Arc Astra Network mobile application
  2. Log in to your account
  3. Go to Profile
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed, the account and all associated personal data are permanently deleted.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile information (email, name, country, date of birth if provided)
  • In-app account data (mining status, rewards, related progress)

Deletion is irreversible. Data removal is completed immediately or within a maximum of 30 days for backup cleanup.

Additional support

If you cannot access the app, you may contact us at support@arcastranetwork.io for assistance.
