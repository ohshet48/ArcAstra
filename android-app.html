Arc Astra Android App

Interactive mining simulation focused on progression, speed and ecosystem participation

App Overview

Arc Astra is a mobile application designed to offer users an engaging mining-style simulation experience. The app focuses on progression mechanics, activity streaks and ecosystem participation.

Mining & Progression

Users interact with a simulated mining system where progress is built over time. Activity, consistency and in-app actions contribute to faster progression and improved performance.

Ads & Speed Boosts

Watching rewarded advertisements is optional and allows users to temporarily increase mining speed. These boosts enhance in-app progression and provide short-term advantages.

Ecosystem Experience

Arc Astra is designed as part of a broader digital ecosystem experience, encouraging long-term engagement, exploration and feature-based progression.

Transparency & Compliance

  • The application does not provide financial services or investment advice
  • In-app progress and balances are part of a simulation-based experience
  • Watching advertisements is optional and never mandatory
  • The app does not guarantee real-world earnings or profits
  • All features described on this page are available directly in the app
