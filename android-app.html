Arc Astra Android App

Simulation-based mining experience designed for engagement and exploration

Application Overview

Arc Astra is a mobile application that provides users with a simulation-based digital mining experience. All displayed balances and progress are virtual and created solely for in-app interaction.

Mining Simulation

The mining system operates entirely as a simulation. It does not represent real cryptocurrency, blockchain assets, or financial instruments of any kind.

Advertising & Speed Boosts

Users may optionally watch rewarded advertisements to activate temporary mining speed boosts. These boosts only affect virtual progression inside the app.

No Financial Value

Arc Astra does not support withdrawals, cash-out mechanisms, or conversion to real-world currency. The app has no investment or financial purpose.

Transparency & Compliance

  • All in-app rewards are virtual and for simulation purposes only
  • Watching ads is optional and not required to use the app
  • No real-world monetary value or earnings are implied
  • All features described here are available directly inside the app
